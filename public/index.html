The Couple About Jeff & Molly

Their Story

It was a warm fall evening the night fate brought Jeff and Molly together. She had been in DC the night before, and was hesitant to hit the town for a second night. Meanwhile, he was bright eyed and bushy tailed for his second night out in Baltimore. She decided though, that since it was a close friend’s birthday she would muster the energy to tackle the night scene one more time. Luck would have it that they had a mutual friend, of whom Jeff was running around with on that indian summer evening. That friend eventually gave Molly a call to hang out with him and a mysterious friend of his own. They met. Sparks flew. And the rest is history.

The Proposal

Molly had been pestering Jeff to take her to a Ballet for years. She knew it was a lost cause, but playfully joked about it again and again, secretly hoping he might cave. On their 3rd anniversary Jeff surprised her with a trip to see the Nutcracker in NYC, with a proud smile on his face, internally boasting about how great of a boyfriend he was for muscling through a ballet. She suspected it was because he had run out of gift ideas. Nevertheless, they made it to the Big Apple in early December, and after sitting through a flawless performance at the Lincoln Center, Jeff dropped to one knee outside of the building, under the bright lights, in the pouring rain. It was perfect. She cried. She said yes. And that is why you are all here.

Accomodations Where to Stay

Le Grande Lodge

Overlooking Lake Ouimet, this Scandinavian-style log resort on 13.5 acres is just off Highway 327 and 5.5 km from Tremblant ski resort.

All of the suites come with a fireplace and a fully equipped kitchen. Suites have 1 or 2 bedrooms, living rooms, and pull-out sofas. Some suites have vaulted ceilings and terraces or private balconies, most with scenic views.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are available at the Chez Borivage restaurant inside the lodge. Casual lunch and dinner fare are also available at the whiskey bar, next to the Chez Borivage. Spa, massages, and a number of relaxing treatments are available. As well as a lifeguard attended semi-Olympic indoor pool and sauna.

We encourage our guests to stay here with us at the lodge, where group rates are available for all of the rooms. Please see the attached form for room rate information, and for email booking. Unfortunately, the group rate is not available via their online booking system, so please call and mention our names, or email the form with payment information.

Thursday and Friday’s events will all be held at the lodge, so the convenience of staying here is huge. We would love to see a lot of our friends and family in one place! However, there are plenty of other accommodations available for you to book.

Activities Things to do during your stay

Skiing

Mont-Tremblant slopes are located in the Tremblant village. The mountain offers 96 trails on four distinct slope sides.

Ski and snowboard storage are available at the lodge, with concierge service for your equipment. A free shuttle to Tremblant Ski Resort departures every 30 minutes between 8 and 5pm. Avoid the lineups by purchasing your lift tickets at the hotel reception, for Tremblant and Mont-Blanc ski resorts. Equipment rental and maintenance available in the hotel boutique.

At the end of the day, enjoy a cup of hot chocolate served from 4 to 6pm, and don't miss Happy Hour at the Whisky Bar with nachos and snacks from 5 to 7pm.

Please visit the tremblant ski page for more details.

The Pedestrian Village

A short shuttle ride from the Lodge, check out Tremblant’s pedestrian village. Dozens of restaurants, bars and shops. Snow tubing, ice skating, snow-mobiling and more!

Please visit the tremblant village page for more details.

Casino de Mont-Tremblant

Located on Versant Soleil at Mont-Tremblant Resort and connected to the pedestrian village on the South Side by a new gondola. Only minutes from Le Grand Lodge, this welcoming, upscale establishment is a sparkling gem in a stunning natural setting.

Please visit the tremblant casino page for more details.
